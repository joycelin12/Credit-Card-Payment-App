Info

This app will help to keep track of all your credit card payments and credit card limits. You will enter your spending amount and spending type in each new record which can be displayed in the graph. You can also enter your payment amount to keep record on the amount paid. The graph will show the total amount owed from all the credit cards after payment deducted against the past week. The recommendation would be spending less than 10% of credit limit.
